Easter Brunch at Philip's House

Course 1 — Pretzel

Shape yeast dough into pretzels, dip in baking soda water, salt the tops, bake until deep brown.

Course 2 — Savory rolls

Fill pastry with seasoned meat or cheese, bake until golden, slice and serve with cut vegetables.

Course 3 — Hot dog

Warm the frank, toast the bun, add mustard, ketchup, and mayo in zig-zags.

Course 4 — Cheeseburger

Cook seasoned beef patties, melt cheese on top, stack with lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles on a bun.

Course 5 — Chicken penne

Boil penne, grill chicken pieces, toss with garlic cream sauce, parsley, and diced tomato.
